# Reviewer Checklist

Before approving a draft follow-up:

- [ ] Transcript is fake/demo data only.
- [ ] Summary is faithful to the transcript.
- [ ] Action item owners are not invented.
- [ ] Deadlines are not invented.
- [ ] Risks and open questions are still visible.
- [ ] FAQ answers are grounded in the source content.
- [ ] AI opportunities are suggestions, not commitments.
- [ ] Approved delivery text is appropriate for internal sharing.
- [ ] `final_markdown_edited` is filled before approving with edits.
- [ ] Notification channel is safe for demo use.
